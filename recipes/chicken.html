Yoshida's Chicken

How to make Yoshida Chicken

Put into gallon ziplock bag your yoshida sauce and boneless chicken thighs and let marinate in the fridge for at least 3 hours

Once marinated remove from the fridge, put chicken onto your gas or charcoal grill at a medium heat

Cook until chicken is at least 165 degrees internal temperature

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken thighs
  • Yoshida's sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place chicken, salt, pepper, and yoshida sauce into a ziplock bag
  2. Let sit for at least 3 hour to marinate in the fridge
  3. Cook over gas/coal bbq until internal temp of 165 degrees
