Brisket

How to make the brisket

To make the brisket you will first trim the brisket ensuring 1/4 inch of fat cap remains. Season the brisket with your favorite rub then put the brisket onto your pellet smoker

The pellet smoker should be at 230 degrees, you will smoke the brisket until an internal temperature of 165 degrees. Once at 165 degrees, you must wrap your brisket with buther paper

Place the brisket back on the smoker and continue cooking until an internal temperature of 203 degrees or until it probes tender.

Ingredients

  • 1 Packer Brisket
  • Favorite Brisket Seasoning
  • Mustard for binder
  • Butcher paper for wrapping

Steps

  1. Trim your brisket
  2. Put mustard on the brisket to act as a binder
  3. Place the brisket on the smoker fat cap up
  4. Smoke until internal temp of 165 degrees
  5. Wrap the brisket
  6. Smoke wrapped until probe tender
  7. Let rest at least 1 hour
